Our story

An agency built around one graph — the one that goes up.

MDM MEHAK started with a simple frustration: agencies that measured success in impressions instead of income. We built the opposite.

Company story

From one client's website to a full growth partner.

MDM MEHAK began by building a single website for a local business that couldn't get its phone to ring. When that changed within weeks, word spread — and the same question kept coming back: "can you do our ads too?" Today we run full-funnel programs — web, ads, SEO and content — for businesses who'd rather see results than sit through another slide deck.

We stayed small enough to keep every account senior-led, and disciplined enough to turn that into a repeatable system: audit, plan, build, launch, optimise, report.
