Overview

What social media advertising means at MDM MEHAK.

Social platforms reward creative that feels native to the feed, not like an ad. We design, write and edit every campaign in-house, then let the data tell us what to scale.

Every campaign is built around a clear funnel — cold audiences into warm retargeting into conversion — so budget compounds instead of resetting every month.

Why businesses need it

  • → Attention is the scarcest resource on social — generic creative gets skipped in under a second.
  • → Retargeting warm audiences converts at multiples of cold-traffic rates.
  • → Platforms reward engagement, so better creative also lowers your cost per result.
